Snake 4 Now!

Rules and Instructions:

    -The snake will begin to move automatically, so be prepared.
    -Use the arrow keys to move the snake up, down, left, or right.
    -The objective of the game is to get the snake as big as possible, so that it can survive the wilderness.
    -Feed red food pellets to the snake.
    -Be careful! If your snake hits any of the corners or goes out of bounds, it’s game over.
    -Be careful! If your snake accidentally collided with itself, it’s game over.
    -Have fun !
